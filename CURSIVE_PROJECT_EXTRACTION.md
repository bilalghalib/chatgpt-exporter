## ðŸ›ï¸ CURSIVE GUILDS - Solving Network Poverty

**The Problem You're Solving**:

> "A system that only helps the well-connected is not a tool for learning; it's a tool for privilege."

> "This is how we solve 'network poverty.' We build a bridge from the 'Private Studio' to a 'Public Workshop.'"

**What is a Guild**:
> "A Guild is a community of practice built around a specific topic ('The Python Guild,' 'The Physics Guild,' 'The Startup Guild'). It's a public space, but it's designed with rules that prevent it from becoming 'Bad Reddit.' It's a workshop, not a forum."

**The Journey of a User with No Network**:

1. **Day 1**: You join Cursive. You have no Circles. You work in your Private Studio. You get stuck on a concept.

2. **Week 1**: You go to the relevant Guild. Anonymously, you post your problem as a "Bounty." You receive three helpful, anonymous notes. You mark the best one.

3. **Month 1**: You start contributing to other people's Bounties. You gain reputation and reach the Journeyman level. People start to recognize your anonymous handle as a source of good ideas.

4. **Month 2**: The system notices you've had several positive interactions with another anonymous user. It prompts you both to form a Circle. You both accept. You now have the beginning of your network.

5. **Year 1**: You are now a high-reputation Artisan in your Guild. You've formed a trusted Study Circle of 4 people you met through the platform. You are invited to become a Guild Mentor.

**The Core Principle**:
> "This system is designed to be the ultimate equalizer. It doesn't matter who you know in the real world. The only thing that matters is the quality of your thinking and your willingness to help others."

## ðŸ—ï¸ TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE - The Five Primitives

**Source**: 2025-09-16 Digital work platform design conversation

### **The Core System** (in Bilal's negotiation with Claude):

**Five Primitives:**
> "Frame Â· Run Â· Commit Â· Whisper Â· Store"

1. **Frame**: Template/structure for a thinking session
2. **Run**: Live session (presence, timers, 20-min rooms)
3. **Commit**: Freezes the result (with epitaph/summary)
4. **Whisper**: AI asking questions inside a Frame (questions-only, no answers)
5. **Store**: Scoped memory - frames read context, commits update it
